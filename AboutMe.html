My Purpose

At my core, I believe in sharing our gifts with others. I am inspired by my research; I am driving by data and I move with faith. I am a great educator. The purpose of this website and all things that I do is to help others find their greatness. My purpose is to connect to universities, school districts, corporations and families

My Experience

I have a bachelor's degree in Mathematics, a master's degree in Education and I am a doctoral student studying Learning and Organizational Change. I have worked as an educator, trainer and educational consultant for over 10 years.

Each year, I attend a variety of webinars and conferences to better understand learners and improve curriculum development. As a presenter, my lectures focus on student engagement, classroom management, data-driven instruction, professionalism, and human resources policies and procedures.

My Background

Math was my first love! As a child, I would make up math problems and run the numbers in my head. If I was ever bored in a room full of people or chairs, I would think of equations to figure out how many people or chairs were in the room. I would do it twice, just to make sure I got the same number each time. Since math was my passion, I decided to become an educator and share my passion with others.
